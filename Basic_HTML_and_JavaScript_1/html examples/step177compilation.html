Wood       Nymphs

Wood nymphs are the spirits of trees. Also known
as Dryads. They would protect the trees they were
tied too, for if the tree died the wood nymphs died
too.

Sea       Nymphs

There were many kinds of sea nymphs. They were defined
by which sea they lived. The Nereds were associated with
the Aegean Sea, where they delt with thier father Nereus.
They were part of Poseidon's entourage
