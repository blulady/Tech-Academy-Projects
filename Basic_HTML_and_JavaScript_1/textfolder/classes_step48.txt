Classes: Properties vs Behaviors
properties (structure that creates a class ie Vehicle)
Behaviors (actions)
Properties of Classes:
structure that creates a class ie Vehicle
energy source: gas vs electric
physical form: sedan vs convertible vs pickup
number of doors: 2 vs 4 vs 5
mechanism of acceleration: accelerator pedal vs speed lever
Behaviors of Classes:
acceleration
breaking
steering right
steering left

Vehicle 1:
